Who I Am

I’m a mechanical engineer focused on designing and releasing robust electromechanical systems into the real world. My work spans early proof-of-concept through production release: CAD, sealing, thermal design, tolerance control, fixture development, PLM, and supplier handoff.

Areas of focus:
• Autonomous mobile robots (sensor integration, structural mounting, serviceability)
• Ground-positioning radar for vehicle localization (environmental sealing, DFMA, thermal path)
• Assembly/test fixtures and process documentation for repeatable builds
• Working directly with manufacturing to transition from prototype to scalable production
